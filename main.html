What TFR does
The company works in the area of applying formal methods, that is mathematical modelling, to the analysis of safety of mission
critical software. We apply static verification techniques and in house tools to effectively overcome state space explosion issues.
Expertise
INDUSTRIAL EXPERIENCE
We have successfully delivered number of projects on safety verification of signalling designs and FM-assisted design pipelines.
RESEARCH
The company was created by the computer science researchers from Newcastle University with a substantial experience in formal verification methods. We actively publish new results.
FORMAL METHOD EXPERTS
The company develops bespoke tools for mathematical modelling of signalling plans, control tables and signalling data.
